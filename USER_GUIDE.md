# ‚ùì FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: I forgot my password?**
A: There's no password! Just use your mobile number and OTP (123456 for demo)

**Q: Can I edit a booking after confirming?**
A: No, but you can cancel PENDING bookings and create a new one

**Q: How do I know my booking is confirmed?**
A: Check notifications and booking status. Green = Accepted

**Q: What's the difference between Accepted and Ongoing?**
A: Accepted = Scheduled, Ongoing = Service currently happening

**Q: Can I register as Admin?**
A: No, admin accounts are pre-configured. Use demo login.

**Q: Why do I need to enter OTP every time?**
A: For security. The demo OTP is always `123456`

**Q: Can I book multiple services at once?**
A: Book one at a time. You can create multiple bookings.
